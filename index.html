Concept by Shane Gibbons Designs

Problem Statement

To give young professionals a cashless way to pay other individuals quickly, easily and securely.

Persona - Brandon

  • Young man who likes to explore his city. Enjoys going out with friends and spends extra money on dinners out, game nights and concerts
  • Does not carry cash and dislikes having to go to the bank
  • Uses Electronic check cashing whenever possible
  • Feels that everything should be handled electronically
  • Often splits expenses with friends, such as food and drink for game nights
  • Being unprepared and having to borrow cash is frustrating
